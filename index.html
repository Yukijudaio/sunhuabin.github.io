BIOGRAPHY

I am Feng Guo (郭枫 in Chinese), M.Eng. Student at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), under the supervision of Prof. Chuan Shi since Fall 2024.. Prior to that, I got my B.Eng. in Computer Science and Technology from Beijing University of Technology (BJUT) in 2024.

My research interests include machine learning and optimization methods with applications in EDA, especially power modeling, design space exploration, and physical design. Also, my research includes data mining and graph neural network
